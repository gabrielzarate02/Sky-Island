The Battle Heard Around the World!

The ruthless Cthulhu has been ravaging throughout Sky Island. The people on the Island decide that enough is enough and plan to fight back on the Cthulhu. The massive beasts prepares to fight back as well. The weather on the island intensifies as the battle grows further an further. Hundreds of men charge towards the monster , hoping to stay alive at the end.
